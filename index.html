📘 Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 Is this location-specific?

+

No. This is a 100% digital business. You can operate it from anywhere in the world with an internet connection. There are no physical location constraints.

🔹 Is this a one-time investment?

+

Yes. You pay once to get your full license, certification, tools, training, and tech access. There are no monthly or hidden charges. You can recover your investment within the first few clients if you follow the roadmap.

🔹 Is this astrology?

+

No. IRIS Analysis is based on neuroscience and brain mapping through iris patterns. It does not involve any predictions, horoscopes, or belief-based systems. It is purely scientific and non-judgmental.

🔹 How accurate?

+

Over 90% accurate. The system has been validated through more than 1,00,000 reports and is continuously refined using AI and real-world feedback. It delivers consistent results across ages and professions.

🔹 Any technical skills required?

+

None at all. You don’t need any prior tech knowledge. Full training will be provided to help you understand and operate the system smoothly. If you can use WhatsApp, you’re already qualified. All backend operations and analysis are automated.

🔹 Can I do it part-time?

+

Yes. Many start part-time and grow as they gain confidence. You can work on your own schedule and scale at your pace.

🔹 Do I meet clients?

+

No. Everything is digital. Reports are generated online and can be delivered via email or WhatsApp. Optional consultations can be done over video calls.

🔹 Can I scale this?

+

Absolutely. You can grow through B2B partnerships, school and HR tie-ups, event stalls, digital marketing, and franchise models.
